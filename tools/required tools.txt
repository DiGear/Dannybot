Dannybot has various tools to power his commands. These tools are not included. You will need to manually add them.

waifu2x-caffe (in its own folder) EXAMPLE: Dannybot\tools\waifu2x-caffe\waifu2x-caffe.exe
UltimateVocalRemover (the original CLI version, in its own folder)
FluidSynth 2.0.0 or higher (put in your PATH)
FFMpeg (put in your PATH)
